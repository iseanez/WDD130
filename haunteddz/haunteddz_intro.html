The Scary Story

The origins of this peculiar fact are lost in the darkness of centuries and continue to be passed from mouth to mouth among the locals whose legends persist in telling that come Halloween, many DataZone workers feel pushed and strangely obliged to decorate their workplaces and dress in the style of the time, which is nothing strange or fantastic given that other companies also do the same on these holidays, but, BUT... legend has it that, somehow, the decorations and costumes in DataZone take on a slightly dark and scary look and take on an appearance that is beyond what human hands can do. Our hiding camera finally managed to take unpublished photos and videos as testimony to this strange event...

Discretion is advised, as much of the material on this page may be disturbing to some people...
